Bourbon

By law, bourbon must be made from a grain mixture that is at least 51 percent corn. Additionally, bourbon is always aged in new charred-oak barrels and distilled to no more than 80 percent ABV.

Proof

Char/Charring

In order to impart smoky flavors and aromas into whiskey, the inner surface of wood barrels (used for aging) are partially burned.
