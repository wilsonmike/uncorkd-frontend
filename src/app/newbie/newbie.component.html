Main Terms

Bourbon

By law, bourbon must be made from a grain mixture that is at least 51 percent corn. Bourbon must be made in the United States. Additionally, bourbon is always aged in new charred-oak barrels and distilled to no more than 80 percent ABV.

Proof

Measurement of beverage alcohol on a scale, in America, of 200. A 100¬∞ proof spirit contains 50 percent alcohol.

Char/Charring

In order to impart smoky flavors and aromas into whiskey, the inner surface of wood barrels (used for aging) are partially burned.

Neat

A drink served "neat" is a single, unmixed liquor served without being chilled and without any water, ice, or other mixer.

On The Rocks

"On the rocks" refers to liquor poured over ice cubes, and a "rocks drink" is a drink served on the rocks.

Some Awesome Cocktails

Mint Julep

Ingredients
-5 to 6 mint leaves
-1/2 oz Simple syrup
-2oz Bourbon
-1/2 oz Dark rum
-Mint to garnish
Instructions
Muddle mint and sugar lightly. Add bourbon and crushed ice. Stir until frost forms on outside of glass. Top with more crushed ice. Float dark rum on top. Garnish with mint (orange wheel and fresh berries optional).

Suffering Bastard

Ingredients
-1oz Bourbon
-1 oz Gin
-1/2 oz lime juice cordial
-2 dashes Aromatic bitters
-4oz Ginger Beer
-Mint to garnish
Instructions
Build all ingredients in shaker except for ginger beer. Add ice and shake until chilled. Strain into Collins or Old Fashioned glass. Top with ginger beer and fresh ice. Garnish with mint, orange wheel optional.

Old Fashioned

Ingredients
-2oz Bourbon
-1 tsp Sugar (1 cube)
-Angostura Bitters(dash to preference)
-Orange peel for garnish
Instructions
Muddle sugar and bitters in glass. Add bourbon and minimal amount of ice and stir. Strain contents into Old Fashioned glass with ice and garnish with orange peel and cherry.

Manhattan

Ingredients
-2oz Bourbon
-1oz Sweet vermouth
-Bitters to taste
-Cherry to garnish
Instructions
Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice, stir. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with a cherry.

Whiskey Sour

Ingredients
-2oz Bourbon
-3/4oz Lemon juice
-3/4oz Simple syrup
-Egg white(optional)
-Cherry and/or lemon wedge for garnish
Instructions Add all ingredients to a shaker and add ice. *If using egg white dry shake first-don't add ice. Strain into rocks glass filled with ice and garnish.
